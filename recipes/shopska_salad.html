Shopska Salad

Description

This is salad is traditional Bulgarian salad, whose colors recall the Bulgarian flag

Ingerdients

  • 2,204623 pounds tomatoes
  • 3 pepers
  • 0.440925 pounds Feta cheese
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 onion
  • half bunch fresh parsley
  • 1 table spoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegitables. Peel the cucumbers and chop them and the tomatoes in cubes.

  2. Peel and cut into small pieces the onion

  3. Cut into to stripes the pepers

  4. Mix all the vegitables together and add grated the grated cheese. Cut the parsley into small pieces and mix everything. Add the olive oil and stir. If you wish you can add more olive oil.
